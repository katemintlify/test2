## Quickstart process overview

1. 1.  
[Create a Snyk account](/getting-started/quickstart/create-a-snyk-account).

2. 2.  
[Set up a Snyk integration](/getting-started/quickstart/set-up-an-integration): to give Snyk access to your code for scanning.

3. 3.  
[Import a Project](/getting-started/quickstart/import-a-project) (or run a scan locally) to scan and identify issues.

4. 4.  
[Review the results](/getting-started/quickstart/view-snyk-scan-results) and take action based on the details of the issues found.
